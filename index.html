Universals, Masks and Lists

I will use terms such as universals, lists, and masks very often. The author of such a triad, as I understand it now, is Roman Mikhailov. Universals are something that cannot be defined in any way, no matter how hard you strive, it is something that cannot be described. These can be various concepts, for example: metaphysics, being, etc.
